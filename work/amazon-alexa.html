“Alexa Game skills Hackathon” is for tech enthusiasts across India to build voice-first experiences in Gaming! This event requires developers to think outside of the traditional development process and embrace a voice-first design approach.
In this project, I have designed two different illustrations to visually demonstrate how people interact with the Alexa Echo and Alexa Button.
Illustration 1:  Illustration represents how Alexa button games are so much fun and competitive to play among friends.
Illustration 2:  Illustration represents how you can play interactive games with Amazon's voice assistant.
